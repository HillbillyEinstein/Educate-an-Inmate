Help an inmate go from Corrections to Corrected

Educate an Inmate would be happy to have you on our team.

Want to change the world?

You can, one woman's life at a time. Being a sponsor is a semester long commitment at the least. It can be an opportunity for you to be a positive light in someone's life. Just the process of going to prison is life changing and life damaging. The women at Mabel Bassett have been locked up and the key thrown away, sometimes forever. You can not only give the gift of a college education, but you could also form a life long bond with a woman who needs it most in this world.

How do I do it?

The first step is to choose a sponsee. Then, you can contact Jami Chambers at MBCC to let her know who you would like to sponsor. She will have the woman enroll at Rose State College. During this process it would be great for you to send an introduction letter to your new sponsee! Once enrolled, it will be your responsibility to set up a payment plan with Terrance Grayson at Rose State. Then it is up to you to decide if you would like to continue being a sponsor for the next semester!

Rose State at MBCC

Rose State has been gracious enough to provide remote learning to Mabel Bassett Correctional Center. As of now, there are four degrees offered.

  1. Business Admin. Associates in Applied Science Degree
  2. Business Associates in Science Degree
  3. Enterprise Development Associate Arts Degree Reach Higher Emphasis
  4. Liberal Studies Associate Arts Degree Interdisciplinary Studies Emphasis
  5. Social Sciences Associate in Arts Degree
