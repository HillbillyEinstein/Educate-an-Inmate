Some women, who are already having a hard time paying for the college classes, lack the funds for books. Thankfully Educate and Inmate has a Donate page where we collect donations from you for books or other college necessities.

Filling in the Gap

Women at Mabel Bassett Correctional Center have a desire to further their education. With the current status of the DOC Education Department, this particular facility does not have access to scholarships or funds for woman who are unable to afford college. Self pay being the only option, women need an outside source to find college funds. Educate an Inmate fills in that gap.
